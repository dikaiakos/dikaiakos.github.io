Rules of "Thumb"

  • Take Community directives at face value.

  • If not clear what they mean, ask!

  • Write "honest" proposals:

    • Have clear goals and go for them
    • Do not over-charge
    • When writing, be punctual and to the point
    • Do not propose just for the sake of it.

  • Consortium formation.

  • Respect deadlines.
