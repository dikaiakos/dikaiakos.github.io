Motivation

  • A paradigm shift: from Client-Server towards more flexible schemes that adapt dynamically to user needs, connection modalities, system resources.

  • A novel software development approach: JAVA-based Mobile-Agent.

  • A framework is required to:

    • study performance issues of mobile-agent-based systems, and
    • compare mobile-agent platforms quantitatively and qualitatively.
