Fundamental Remarks

  • Digital Convergence has led to:

    • Internet becoming a universal information infrastructure.
    • Explosion of systems and services available on Internet/Web.

  • Next-generation Internet systems and services guided by:

    • Current technological trends: integrating wireless connectivity; exploiting mobile, hand-held and special devices.
    • Emerging requirements and needs of Internet use: seamless service provision to mobile users, addressing information overload problems, improving performance and QoS, providing security, safeguarding user privacy, etc.
