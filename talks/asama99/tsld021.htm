Microbenchmarks

  • Key software components:

    • Mobile Agents to materialize modules of C/S, C/A/S, etc.
    • Messenger Agents for flexible communication.
    • Messaging for efficient communication and synchronization.

  • [AC/L]: measures overhead of creating-launching agents.

  • [MSG]: measures messaging overhead.

  • [ROAM]: measures agent-travelling overhead.

  • [SYNCH]: measures synchronization overhead.

  • Parameterized by the number of iterations they execute.

  • Metric: total time to completion.
