Software Models for Mobile Computing

  • Client-server model inadequate for wireless and mobile applications.

  • Extensions of the Client-Server model provide for the requirements of wireless and mobile applications:

    • Client-Agent-Server (C/A/S)
    • Client-Intercept-Server (C/I/S)

  • Mobile-agent Model.

  • Adaptations of C/A/S and C/I/S to the mobile-agent paradigm.
