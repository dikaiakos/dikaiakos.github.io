Remarks on Agent Dispatch

  • Concordia server outperforms Aglets in agent-dispatch:

    • Concordia dispatches an image of an agent being transported; the image retracts its classes from the sender on a need-to-know basis.
    • Aglets Workbench dispatches an Aglet together with all the objects reachable from this Aglet.
    • Additional overhead may be incurred by Aglets due to the extra level of indirection introduced by their callback model.

  • Average performance of Concordia improves even more over Aglets, with subsequent dispatches of the same agent.
