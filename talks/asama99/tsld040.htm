Discussion

  • Aglets outperform Concordia for the first query.

  • Concordia/s performance is substantially improved over Aglets for subsequent queries.

  • These results agree with observations from benchmarks [AC/L], [ROAM] and [SYNCH].

  • Messages are more efficient than messenger agents, although the latter offer greater flexibility.

  • Concordia/s Service Bridges give the best performance results. This approach, however, lacks the flexibility of dynamically “parking” at the database a agent.
