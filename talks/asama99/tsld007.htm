Why Mobile Agents?

  • Programmability: Modularity, Flexibility and Adaptability; Easy integration of heterogeneous information; Monitoring and notification; Intelligence; Security & Robustness.

  • Performance: Efficient distributed database access over the Web; Distributed information retrieval and filtering; Reduction of network load.

  • Autonomy: Asynchronous execution; Support for weak connectivity and disconnected operation; Information dissemination; Adaptability to various connection modalities.
