Summary and Future Work

  • Quantitative evaluation of MA-based systems entails:

    • identifying appropriate software models and anticipated applications
    • defining microbenchmarks and application kernels

  • We identified software models pertinent to Web-database applications and developed corresponding benchmarks.

  • Microbenchmark results provide insights into the performance traits of MA platforms and help us explain the behavior of application kernels.

  • We are working on expanding models and benchmarks.
