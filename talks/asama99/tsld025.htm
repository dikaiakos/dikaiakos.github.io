Agent Dispatch from Applets: remarks

  • IBM’s Aglets Workbench outperforms Concordia when dispatching Agents from “agent-aware” applets.

  • Aglets Workbench supports interoperability with applets through IBM’s Fiji Applet and Plug-in.

  • This approach is apparently more optimized than the Concordia Applet, which required a manual hack to send and receive agents.
