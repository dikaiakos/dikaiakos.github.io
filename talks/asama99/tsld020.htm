Benchmarking MA Systems

  • Microbenchmarks: short codes designed to isolate and measure performance properties of basic, “low level” components of mobile-agent-based systems.

  • Application kernels: short, synthetic codes designed to measure performance properties of interesting application frameworks.

  • Full application codes: analyze the performance behavior of full-blown, “killer” applications, working under realistic workloads.
