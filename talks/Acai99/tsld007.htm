WHY FOCUSING ON FINANCIAL INFORMATION?

  • There is a widening interest for investment information and services over Internet.

  • A variety of financial information sources and investment services on Internet.

  • Enormous amounts of information.

  • Information provision involves a variety of data formats.

  • Timeliness of information can be critical.

  • Resource-cost constraints often dictate the provision of data.

  • Need to cope with user mobility.
