PORTFOLIO MONITORING REQUIREMENTS

  • Provide users with local “views” of the global information space, pertinent to user interests.

  • Collect information on user behalf, on a continuous basis and according to user interests.

  • Feed information to users selectively, adapting to:

    • their priorities
    • the urgency of the information
    • connection modalities

  • Allow users to record and update their changing interests and priorities via “user-centered” interfaces.
