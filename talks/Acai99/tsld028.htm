CONCLUSIONS AND FUTURE WORK

  • Next-generation systems for financial information dissemination on Internet will entail:

    • a mediator-based architecture enhancing off-line information gathering and intelligent filtering
    • the intelligent regulation of information flow to the user
    • enhanced methods for registration of user interests
    • seamless support to mobile users

  • Java-based Mobile Agents represent a promising platform for developing such systems and easily adapting them to emerging technologies for information dissemination and integration.

  • We are currently investigating the employment of XML in FIGI.
